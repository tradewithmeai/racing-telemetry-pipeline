IMPORTANT: Place your track image here!

Filename: track.png (or track.jpg)

Requirements:
- Overhead/satellite view of Barber Motorsports Park
- PNG or JPG format
- Recommended size: 1920x1080 or larger
- Should show the full track layout

The coordinate transformer will automatically map GPS coordinates from the telemetry
data to pixel positions on this image.

You can find satellite imagery from:
- Google Maps (screenshot)
- Google Earth
- Racing circuit official websites
- iRacing track maps

Once you have the image, rename it to "track.png" and place it in this directory.
